Cycle across the Old Continent from Scandinavia to Rome, or from Lisbon to Moscow, by choosing one of the 19 bike routes of EuroVelo. EuroVelo is a network of long-distance cycling routes criss-crossing Europe, for a total length of 90,000 km. EuroVelo is a project of the European Cyclists' Federation (ECF).
